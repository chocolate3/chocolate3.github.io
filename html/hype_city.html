Hype City Soundtrack

Neutral Milk Hotel (1993)

The Hype City Soundtrack was Neutral Milk Hotel’s first “real” album. It was actually released unlike the other demo tapes of it’s time. You can hear a sound only vaguely reminiscent of Aeroplane or even Avery Island. You do get to hear early versions of a lot of those songs though, like Up and Over, which is just an early version of King of Carrot Flowers Part 3. You’ll no doubt have heard about it or it’s even earlier version, Synthetic Flying Machine. Brilliant stuff, either way. Give it a listen.
