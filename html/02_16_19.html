This was the first gig I ever played. I decided in January that I’d like to have a small show someday. I realized I’d never do it unless I picked a date. February 16th was chosen. I practiced and wrote a setlist and did everything I could, yet the most I learned was the day of. I was with Bradlee G. and she recorded some rehearsal footage. There’s also a tape of it somewhere. Around 10 people showed up at 6:00, but many were late, so I started at 6:30. I played through my set, making Lucy Sonsalla play some music between the two sets. I came back on around 7:10, and I played the other songs. I still had a ton of time, though. I ended up just picking random songs I knew. The highlight of the show was the end of the song Gardenhead/Leave me Alone, King of Carrot Flowers, April 8th, and The Banjo Song.
